NEED ANSWERS?

1. Do I have to supply the photos and graphics for this service? a. No. We have a fully staffed design team that will present to you unlimited choices for graphic designs and background photos.

2. How can I be sure that my business cards will reflect the proper information for contact to my website? a. That is part of the design effort we put forth. We have design business cards that are attractive and contain all the information to lead you customers to you.

3. Do you send the artwork to my printer for printing? a. We can do that, but we have the product available from our sources at very competitive pricing.

4. Do you have a free trial available? a. You can use the website for 30 days absolutely free.

5. Can my new website be product specific? a. You may set up varied sites for the purpose of introducing new products, new employees and promotions.

6. Can I change my website? a. Your introductory site can be changed as often as you desire. Our experienced designers can customize your site as often as you desire.

7. How many pages can I have on my website? a. You can include as many pages as you desire, but the focus for introductory sites is to be brief, to the point, and lead customers to your main site.
