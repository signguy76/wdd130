Overview

Hi. Have you noticed these days that it has become hard to get a proper introduction with potential clients that helps them to know who you are and what you do? I work with website development and have found one of the best ways to achieve a proper introduction is by way of an introductory site. With the proper presentation in an introductory website, you have an opportunity to spark the curiosity of potential clients. On many occasions when I have only a moment to introduce myself, I have given a business card with my website and received replies to my online introduction. Do you currently have an introductory site?

Audience

The audience for this site is focused on people/companies that want to get their Name out to potential customers. This site could be viewed as a source for an electronic business card. Its purpose is not to sell product but to introduce their company to send them to a more comprehensive site.

[Marketing]

Website size. These mini-websites are designed to ‘get the attention’ of you potential customers. Within the website there are plenty of opportunities for your customers to link to your main website. Brevity is often the key to getting someone’s attention. It is a lot like reading a resume, if a resume is more than a couple of pages long, they are often set aside and ignored.

How it works. Use this to get the attention of your customers. With a few well-placed graphics and select verbiage, you can prompt them to look deeper into your services. All too often when we have a brief encounter with a new contact, the time is too short to make a formal introduction. Sometimes there is just enough time to hand off a card. As you hand your new “Get To Know You” business card include an invitation to visit the site on the back of the card. This is your opportunity to extend the short encounter to share more information about your services.

Business card marketing. Introduce your new website by handing your business card to your potential customers. A properly designed card will be attractive and have all the contact information to get in touch with your new introductory website. Let us help you design your introductory business card. Your card will have the URL information to find you and will be accompanied by QR codes that will give instant access to your site.

FAQ

1. Do I have to supply the photos and graphics for this service? a. No. We have a fully staffed design team that will present to you unlimited choices for graphic designs and background photos.

2. How can I be sure that my business cards will reflect the proper information for contact to my website? a. That is part of the design effort we put forth. We have design business cards that are attractive and contain all the information to lead you customers to you.

3. Do you send the artwork to my printer for printing? a. We can do that, but we have the product available from our sources at very competitive pricing.

4. Do you have a free trial available? a. You can use the website for 30 days absolutely free.

5. Can my new website be product specific? a. You may set up varied sites for the purpose of introducing new products, new employees and promotions.

6. Can I change my website? a. Your introductory site can be changed as often as you desire. Our experienced designers can customize your site as often as you desire.

7. How many pages can I have on my website? a. You can include as many pages as you desire, but the focus for introductory sites is to be brief, to the point, and lead customers to your main site.
